Your privacy is critically important to us. At City Golf League, LLC we have a few fundamental principles:

  • We don’t ask you for personal information unless we truly need it.
  • We don’t share your personal information with anyone except to comply with the law, develop our products, or protect our rights.
  • We don’t store personal information on our servers unless required for the on-going operation of our services.
  • In our messaging system, we aim to make it as simple as possible for you to control what’s visible to the public, seen by search engines, kept private, and permanently deleted.

Below is our privacy policy which incorporates these goals:

It is City Golf League, LLC policy to respect your privacy regarding any information we may collect while operating our website.

City Golf League, LLC only discloses logged in user and commenter IP addresses under the same circumstances that it uses and discloses personally identifying information as described below. Users may from time-to-time forward personal messages that they deem offensive or inappropriate to the administrator. Cookies

A cookie is a string of information that a website stores on a visitor’s computer, and that the visitor’s browser provides to the website each time the visitor returns. The primary use of cookies by City Golf League, LLC is intended to enhance the user experience on the website. For example, the cookies will limit the numbers of times you see the “register for league play” banner on the home page. Cookies are also used to help City Golf League, LLC identify and track visitors, their usage of the City Golf League, LLC website, and their website access references. City Golf League, LLC visitors who do not wish to have cookies placed on their computers should set their browsers to refuse cookies before using City Golf League, LLC website, with the drawback that certain features of City Golf League, LLC website may not function properly without the aid of cookies.
