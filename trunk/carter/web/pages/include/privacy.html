YOUR PRIVACY IS CRITICALLY IMPORTANT TO US. AT OPEN PLAY TENNIS WE HAVE A FEW FUNDAMENTAL PRINCIPLES:

  • We don't ask you for personal information unless we truly need it.
  • We don't share your personal information with anyone except to comply with the law, develop our products, or protect our rights.
  • We don't store personal information on our servers unless required for the on-going operation of our services.
  • In our messaging system, we aim to make it as simple as possible for you to control what's visible to the public, seen by search engines, kept private, and permanently deleted.

Below is our privacy policy which incorporates these goals:

It is Open Play Tennis policy to respect your privacy regarding any information we may collect while operating our website.

WEBSITE VISITORS

Like most website operators, Open Play Tennis collects non-personally identifying information of the sort that web browsers and servers typically make available, such as the browser type, language preference, referring site, and the date and time of each visitor request. The purpose in collecting non-personally identifying information is to better understand how Open Play Tennis visitors use its website.

From time to time, Open Play Tennis may release non-personally identifying information in the aggregate, e.g., by publishing a report on trends in the usage of its website.

Open Play Tennis also collects potentially personally identifying information like Internet Protocol (IP) addresses for logged in users and for users leaving comments on the messaging system. Open Play Tennis only discloses logged in user and commenter IP addresses under the same circumstances that it uses and discloses personally identifying information as described below. Users may from time-to-time forward personal messages that they deem offensive or inappropriate to the administrator. The administrator of Open Play Tennis will view said messages only to determine if a user has violated the USE OF Public and Private message Systems as outlined in the Terms of Use. The above example is the only instance that the administrator of Open Play Tennis or any of its employees will view personal messages.

GATHERING OF PERSONALLY IDENTIFYING INFORMATION

Certain visitors to Open Play Tennis websites choose to interact with Open Play Tennis in ways that require Open Play Tennis to gather personally identifying information. The amount and type of information Open Play Tennis gathers depends on the nature of the interaction. For example, we ask visitors who register for Open Play Tennis to provide a username, email address and phone number. Those who participate in Open Play Tennis league play are asked to provide additional information, including as necessary the personal and financial information required to process the transaction. In each case, Open Play Tennis collects such information only insofar as is necessary or appropriate to fulfill the purpose of the visitor's interaction with Open Play Tennis. Open Play Tennis does not disclose personally identifying information other than as described below. User's phone numbers are never disclosed and are only used if the administrator or other employee of Open Play Tennis needs to contact a user of Open Play Tennis. Visitors can always refuse to supply personally identifying information, with the caveat that it may prevent them from engaging in certain website-related activities.

AGGREGATED STATISTICS

Open Play Tennis may collect statistics about the behavior of visitors to its website. Open Play Tennis may display this information publicly or provide it to others. However, Open Play Tennis does not disclose personally identifying information other than as described below.

COOKIES

A cookie is a string of information that a website stores on a visitor's computer, and that the visitor's browser provides to the website each time the visitor returns. The primary use of cookies by Open Play Tennis is intended to enhance the user experience on the website. For example, the cookies will limit the numbers of times you see the register for league play banner on the home page. Cookies are also used to help Open Play Tennis identify and track visitors, their usage of the Open Play Tennis website, and their website access references. Open Play Tennis visitors who do not wish to have cookies placed on their computers should set their browsers to refuse cookies before using Open Play Tennis website, with the drawback that certain features of Open Play Tennis website may not function properly without the aid of cookies.

You acknowledge that such transfers may occur, and that any acquirer of Open Play Tennis may continue to use your personal information as set forth in this policy.

ADS

Ads appearing on any of our websites may be delivered to users by advertising partners, who may set cookies. These cookies allow the ad server to recognize your computer each time they send you an online advertisement to compile information about you or others who use your computer. This information allows ad networks to, among other things, deliver targeted advertisements that they believe will be of most interest to you. This Privacy Policy covers the use of cookies by Open Play Tennis and does not cover the use of cookies by any advertisers.
