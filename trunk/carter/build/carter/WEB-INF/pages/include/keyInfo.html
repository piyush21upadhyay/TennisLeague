Scheduling Matches

Log on to City Golf League and send your opponent a message indicating you want to schedule your match. Click on send a message under your opponent's Player Profile Page. You can access this either from the members section or by opening your schedule and clicking on your opponent's name.

Handicaps up to date?

Make sure both you and your opponent have current handicaps entered in the Player Profile section. This is very important as your handicap determines who receives strokes and where they are taken. Click here to check.

City Golf League Tips

Every player should print the CGL Rules and keep them on hand for reference during match play. You should also print the scorecard from the City Golf League website to avoid any confusion as to who receives strokes and on what holes strokes are taken. To access the scorecard, click on Print Card under the schedule section of the website.
